Why learning Swahili through kilimanjaro card?

Swahili language is the lingua franca of the African great lakes regions and other parts of the eastern and southern Africa, with its origin in East Africa. Swahili speakers spread more than 14 countries: Tanzania, Uganda, Burundi, Kenya , Rwanda, Democratic Repuplic of Congo(DRC), South Sudan, Somalia, Malawi, Comoro, Zambia, Mozambique and as far as Oman and Yemen in Middle East.

The Swahili language is among the ten(10) most spoken languages in the world with over 250 million speakers. It is the first and so far the only African language to be feted by the UN which has designated 7th July as the world Swahili language Day in compliance of the fact that on 7th July 1954, the Tanganyika African national Union(TANU), the ruling party of then Tanganyika led by Mwalimu Julius Kambarage Nyerere declared Swahili as an important tool in the fight for independence.

The Swahili language also is an offical language of African Union(AU). Therefore it is a necessary move to make in learning the Swahili language for easier interactive communication. Kilimanjaro card is the most simplified method of learning Swahili vocabulary which is designed to escalate comprehension of the Swahili language with effective outcomes.
