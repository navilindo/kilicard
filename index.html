Kilimanjaro Card is a combination of 36 cards through which different games can be played for the purpose of entertainment and education (particularly learning the Swahili language).

Why the name Kilimanjaro?

Kilimanjaro is the Swahili word which means the White Mountain, it is the highest mountain in Africa and the highest free standing mountain in the world located in Tanzania where the standard dialect of Swahili language resides.
