How to Play Kilimanjaro Card?

By using Kilimanjaro Card and Banga Board, you can play different games, namely BASO, KOTEA and NJAMA.

Banga Board is a board on which Kilimanjaro Card games are played. Banga board consists of 58 squares whereas the first row on both player’s side consists of 9 squares with uniform pattern of colour from one square to another, the 4 squares at the center mark the summit of the board presented by the peak of Mount Kilimanjaro on both sides and the remaining square are plain.

Note that, in all Kilimanjaro card’s games, the special cards represent white colour.
