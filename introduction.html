Kilimanjaro Cards Appearance

The total number of cards are 36 categorized into seven groups namely noun cards, verb cards, special cards, pronoun card, adjective card, adverb card and sentence card.

Each card has a backside and frontside appearance.

The Backside Appearance

It presents the picture of Mount kilimanjaro, giraffe and the vegetation around the mountain.


The Frontside Appearance



The frontside of the twenty four (24) noun cards (letter A to Z)

  • i. They have different number of Swahili-English word pairs arranged in alphabetical order from A to Z.
  • ii. Each card contains a picture that represents the object of the first Swahili-English word pair.

The frontside of the four (4) verb cards.

  • 1. They have six (6) Swahili-English word pairs (verbs).
  • ii. Each card contains a picture that describes the verb of the first Swahili-English word pair.


The frontside of the four (4) special cards.

They contain pictures of Star, Sun, Moon and Bulb, whereas the Sun, the Moon and Star naturally produce white light and the bulb produces the white light artificially.
Note that, the special cards represent white colour.
